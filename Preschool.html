SEKOLAH AGAMA RAKYAT (KAFA) FELDA TRIANG 3

I started studying at Sekolah Agama Rakyat (KAFA) Felda Triang 3 or its abbreviation, SAR KAFA Triang 3 at the age of five. This is because my mother worked here as an ustazah, and I had to follow her. As a result, I entered school at an early age. This school is located 16.2km and 16 minutes by car from my house. Here, I studied the grade one syllabus because this school only provides lessons for grades one to five. Therefore, all of my classmates were three years older than me. The following year, my mother was transferred to work at Sekolah Agama Rakyat (KAFA) Felda Mayam, causing me to also transfer schools.

SEKOLAH AGAMA RAKYAT (KAFA) FELDA MAYAM

I studied here from the age of 6 until 11. I studied the first grade syllabus twice more after studying at SAR (KAFA) Felda Triang 3. This school is located 7.8km and takes 9 minutes from my house. This is where I took the UPKK exam in grade 5. To prepare for the exam, I had to attend extra classes held by teachers who taught every weekday. I obtained 8A in the exam, meaning straight A. The subjects tested consisted of the subjects of al-Quran, Ulum Syariah, Sirah, Jawi and Khat, Arabic Language, Akhlak dan Islamiah, Penghayatan Cara Hidup Islam (PCHI) and AmaliSolat.

PASTI AL-FALAH FELDA KUMAI

I studied here when I was 4 and 5 years old. It operates under the umbrella of PASTI (Pusat Asuhan Tunas Islam), which is known for providing Islamic-based preschool education in Malaysia. It is located close to my house, only 1.1km and 2 minutes by car. Here, I was exposed to Quranic memorization (Tahfiz) as it combines basic religious and academic curriculum such as Al-Quran/Iqra', Hadith, Fiqh, Aqidah, Akhlak, Bahasa Melayu, Bahasa English, Mathematics, and Science. For example, I was required to memorize short surahs, and daily prayers. I was also taught practical ablution & prayer.

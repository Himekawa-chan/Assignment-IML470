SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN FELDA KUMAI

I started school here in 2012 when I was 6 years old because the Preschool was located in this school. I attended this school with my brother who was 9 years old at the time. During the school's annual sports event in 2016, I participated in the shot put event, and got third place in the 'Level 2' category consisting of grades 4 to 6. When I was in grade 6, I was appointed to be a school resource center supervisor. I work in the library every Thursday during recess. Academically, I have managed to be in the top 3 in the final examinations in 2014, 2017 and 2018. I also sat for a major examination, the Primary School Assessment Test (UPSR) in 2018. This exam is very important in determining which high school I will enter in the future. As a result of my hard work, attending all the extra classes held by the teachers day and night, I finally managed to get 3A results and 3B.

SEKOLAH MENENGAH KEBANGSAAN AGAMA TENGKU AMPUAN HAJJAH AFZAN PAHANG (a.k.a SMKA TAHAP)

This school is located in Jerantut, Pahang, which is 107km and 1 hour 48 minutes from my house by car. This school requires all the students to live in the dormitory from form 1 to form 6. I started school here in 2019 until 2023/2024.

In this school, I was in the Life Sciences major. I'am also appointed to be part of the MPP in the Student Leader (KP) unit during Form 3 to Form 5. During Form 4, the MPP is likened to the backbone of the school. We are the ones who help, organize and manage the programs in the school such as the Form 1 orientation program, Teacher's Day, and Independence Day. I gained a lot of experience while handling these programs with other MPP friends. I have also been appointed to be a naqibah usrah for the 2023/2024 school session.

In terms of academics, each form has an extra class every day, especially Forms 4 and 5 to prepare us to face the Malaysian Certificate of Education (SPM) which will determine our future. As a result of studying hard almost all the time, I have obtained quite encouraging results (2A+ 2A 2A- 2B+ 3B). As for co-curriculum, I have participated in the Jerantut district-level woodball competition 2022. I have also participated in various programs such as the 'Pahang State-level School Reform Leadership Nadwah (NKISS) 2022, 'Jerantut District Liaison PPIM Leadership Basic Camp', 'Malaysia National Chemistry Quiz', 'National Level Young Stemist Expo', 'STEM Challenge' and 'Kanggaroo Math Competition'.
